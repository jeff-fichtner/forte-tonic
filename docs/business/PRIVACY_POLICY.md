# Privacy Policy for Tonic Music Program Registration System

**Effective Date:** August 4, 2025  
**Last Updated:** August 4, 2025

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Tonic Music Program Registration System ("Tonic," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our student registration automation system for after-school lessons and programs.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Student Information
- Student names and identification numbers
- Grade levels and academic information
- Contact information (addresses, phone numbers)
- Program enrollment and registration details
- Attendance records
- Instrument preferences and class selections

### 2.2 Parent/Guardian Information
- Names and contact information
- Email addresses for communication
- Emergency contact details
- Billing and payment information (if applicable)

### 2.3 Instructor Information
- Instructor names and professional details
- Contact information
- Teaching schedules and availability
- Qualifications and certifications

### 2.4 Technical Information
- Login credentials and authentication data
- System usage logs and access records
- IP addresses and browser information
- Session data for system functionality

## 3. How We Use Information

### 3.1 Primary Purposes
- **Registration Management:** Process and manage student enrollments in music programs
- **Communication:** Send program updates, schedules, and important notifications to parents and students
- **Scheduling:** Coordinate lesson times, instructor assignments, and room bookings
- **Attendance Tracking:** Monitor student participation and progress
- **Administrative Operations:** Maintain accurate records for program administration

### 3.2 Secondary Purposes
- **System Improvement:** Analyze usage patterns to enhance system functionality
- **Compliance:** Meet legal and regulatory requirements for educational programs
- **Safety:** Ensure student safety and appropriate supervision during programs

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 Limited Sharing
We only share information with:
- **Authorized Staff:** School administrators, instructors, and support staff who need access to perform their duties
- **Parents/Guardians:** Information about their own children's participation and progress
- **Students:** Age-appropriate access to their own registration and schedule information

### 4.2 No Third-Party Sales
We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

### 4.3 Required Disclosures
We may disclose information when required by:
- Legal obligations or court orders
- Emergency situations involving student safety
- Compliance with educational regulations

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Storage Location
- Data is stored securely using Google Sheets with enterprise-grade security
- System hosted on secure cloud infrastructure (Render.com)
- Regular backups and data redundancy measures in place

### 5.2 Security Measures
- **Authentication:** Secure login systems with operator-level access controls
- **Encryption:** Data transmission protected by industry-standard encryption
- **Access Controls:** Role-based permissions limiting data access to authorized personnel only
- **Regular Updates:** System security patches and updates applied regularly

### 5.3 Data Retention
- Student records maintained for the duration of program participation
- Historical data retained for administrative and compliance purposes
- Inactive records archived according to institutional policies
- Data permanently deleted upon request when legally permissible

## 6. User Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access Rights
- Parents and eligible students may request access to their personal information
- Right to review and verify accuracy of stored data
- Ability to request corrections to inaccurate information

### 6.2 Communication Preferences
- Option to manage email communication preferences
- Ability to opt-out of non-essential communications
- Right to receive important safety and program notifications

### 6.3 Data Portability
- Right to request data in a portable format when technically feasible
- Assistance with data transfer for legitimate educational purposes

## 7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

### 7.1 Parental Consent
- Parental consent obtained before collecting information from children under 13
- Parents have the right to review their child's information
- Parents may request deletion of their child's information

### 7.2 Limited Collection
- We collect only information necessary for program participation
- No behavioral tracking or profiling of minor students
- Age-appropriate privacy protections implemented

## 8. Cookies and Tracking

### 8.1 Essential Cookies
- Session cookies for system functionality and authentication
- Security cookies to prevent unauthorized access
- No third-party advertising or tracking cookies

### 8.2 Analytics
- Basic usage analytics to improve system performance
- No personal identification in analytics data
- Option to disable non-essential tracking

## 9. Data Breach Response

### 9.1 Incident Response
- Immediate assessment and containment of any security incidents
- Notification to affected parties within 72 hours when required by law
- Coordination with relevant authorities and institutions

### 9.2 Prevention Measures
- Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
- Staff training on data protection best practices
- Continuous monitoring for suspicious activities

## 10. International Data Transfers

Data processing occurs within secure, compliant cloud infrastructure. Any international transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards and comply with applicable privacy laws.

## 11. Policy Updates

### 11.1 Notification

- Updated policies posted on the system with effective dates
- Continued use constitutes acceptance of updated terms

### 11.2 Review Schedule
This privacy policy is reviewed annually and updated as necessary to reflect changes in:
- System functionality and features
- Legal and regulatory requirements
- Best practices in data protection
